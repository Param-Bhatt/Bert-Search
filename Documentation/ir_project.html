IS F469 Information Retrieval Assignment I



  1. Using the knowledge gained from the classes, we made a domain specific search engine. We have implemented an Information Retrieval system to search for relevant emails from a dataset of Enron Corporation. Their emails were leaked into the public and it was reviled that they were a fraud organisation.
    We chose this dataset as it had a lot of emails and they were generic and formal which gave us a well paved way to find a lot of words in our pretrained embeddings corpus.


  2. We used the concept of embeddings where each word is assigned in a multi-dimensional vector space. The vector corresponding for each word depicts it's semantic meaning. It is better at representing the meanings of the words than TF-IDF or any other classical method.


  3. We used embeddings which were learned by deep learning models. The embeddings models are explained further.

    • BERT :- Bi-Directional Encoder Representation from Transformers is a deep learning model that focuses on attention to learn and understand embeddings. It has learned the embeddings in a 768-d vector space.

    • GLOVE :- Global Vectors for Word Representation is a model for distributed word representations learned from unsupervised learning where the distance between the embeddings of the words maps to their semantic similarity. It comes in various dimensional spaces such as 50-d,100-d, etc. We have used 300-d space which is the largest space given by GLOVE.
